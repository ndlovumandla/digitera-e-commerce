Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Digitera

Buying on Digitera

To purchase a digital product, browse our marketplace, click on the product you want, and click "Add to Cart" or "Buy Now". You'll be taken to our secure checkout where you can pay with your credit card, debit card, or EFT. After successful payment, you'll receive an email with download links.

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), EFT transfers, and SnapScan. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Due to the nature of digital products, we generally don't offer refunds once you've downloaded the files. However, if there's a technical issue with the product or it doesn't match the description, please contact our support team within 48 hours for assistance.

After purchase, you'll receive an email with download links. You can also access all your purchased products from your account dashboard under "My Purchases". Downloads are available for 30 days after purchase.

Selling on Digitera

Simply sign up for a creator account, complete your profile, and start uploading your digital products. We'll review your application within 24-48 hours. There are no upfront costs to become a creator.

We charge a 15% commission on all sales, which covers payment processing, hosting, marketing, and customer support. You keep 85% of every sale. There are no monthly fees or listing fees.

Payments are processed weekly on Fridays via EFT to your registered South African bank account. You need to earn at least R100 before a payout is triggered. You can track your earnings in real-time from your creator dashboard.

You can sell various digital products including templates, graphics, photos, music, courses, eBooks, software, and more. All products must be original or properly licensed, and comply with our content guidelines.

Use high-quality preview images, write detailed descriptions, choose appropriate tags, price competitively, and encourage customer reviews. Check out our Creator Resources page for detailed tips and best practices.

Payments & Billing

Yes, all payment information is encrypted and processed through PCI-compliant payment processors. We never store your full credit card details on our servers. Your financial information is completely secure.

Yes, VAT is included in all prices displayed on our platform. As a registered VAT vendor in South Africa, we collect and remit VAT to SARS as required by law. Your invoice will show the VAT breakdown.

Yes, you'll automatically receive a tax invoice via email after every purchase. You can also download invoices from your account dashboard under "Purchase History". All invoices are SARS-compliant.

Technical Support

We support most common file formats including PDF, ZIP, JPG, PNG, PSD, AI, EPS, MP3, MP4, MOV, DOC, PPT, and more. Maximum file size is 500MB per product. If you need to upload larger files, contact our support team.

If you're having download issues, try using a different browser or clearing your browser cache. Make sure you're logged into the correct account. If problems persist, contact our support team with your order number for immediate assistance.

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
