Best Practices

Proven strategies from successful South African creators

Create High-Quality Product Descriptions

Your product description is your sales pitch. Make it compelling and detailed. Include what customers will get, how it helps them, and what makes it unique.

Tips:

  • • Use bullet points for key features
  • • Include file formats and dimensions
  • • Mention software compatibility
  • • Add usage examples

Use High-Quality Preview Images

Your preview images are the first thing customers see. Make them professional and showcase your product effectively.

Best practices:

  • • Use 1200x800px or higher resolution
  • • Show the product in use or context
  • • Include multiple angles or examples
  • • Use consistent branding

Price Competitively

Research similar products and price competitively. Consider the South African market and purchasing power.

Pricing guidelines:

  • • Simple templates: R50 - R200
  • • Complex designs: R200 - R500
  • • Course materials: R300 - R1000
  • • Bundles: 20-30% discount
